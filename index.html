About Me

MBD or Made By Danny is a small site Made By Danny that started on May 10 2024 by a 12 year-old kid named Danny. I (Danny) loved tech. I wanted to learn to code websites (HTML) . I found a short show on a Microsoft site then expanded to W3Schools and youtube where Techno Time showed me how to use Cloudflare. The story has just begun.


Why UK?

You might be asking why Made By Danny UK, that's because I am British and because Cloudflare .uk domains are cheaper. Mainly because madebydanny.com was taken. And I like the sound of "Made By Danny UK" it stands out.


MBD + Cloudflare
