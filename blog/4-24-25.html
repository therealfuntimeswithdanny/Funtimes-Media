No more Linktree time to switch to Buffer

Published on April 22, 2025 | #SocialMedia #Bluesky

After using Linktree for over a year I found out Buffer’s Start Page is better and works with my workflow!

Linktree the link in bio service Is your own custom “card” styled the way you want with social links, a bio, videos and more! Buffer’s start page is the same thing but it’s by buffer!

If you don’t know what a buffer is, it’s a platform where you can schedule posts to social media sites like instagram, threads, feabook, bluesky etc. I use Buffer to schedule posts every day at 9:30 am on my bluesky account @madebydanny.uk!

Since the Start Page is by Buffer I don't need to leave buffer.com to edit it, I can also schedule a post to my start page from the buffer dashboard! One of the main things I like about Start Page is branding and price. All you see on my Start Page is a small test that says “buffer.com”, it’s also free so I don't need to pay!
