The iPhone 14, 3 years later

Published on April 28, 2025 | #Apple #iPhone

Apple announced the iPhone 14 three years ago; how does it work today!

For all 5 of you that asked that question I have the answer. I had my iPhone 14 Product Red for the past year. I got as a promo form Verizon, after my iPhone 13 was stolen.

The iPhone 14 is 6.1-inch iPhone with a Dual Camera system powered by the Apple A15 Bionic chip. Since it has the A15 it can run the latest iOS versions (as of writing the latest iOS update is iOS 18.5 Beta 2).

My iPhone 14 works fine, it’s still fast and responsive and it looks awesome, but compared to my Nikon (D3100) it looks pretty good. It fits perfectly in my hand, and it never gets hot or warm. It only warms up if I’m streaming large videos over my VPN (Tailscale) from a server at my house our backing up videos/photos and iCloud Backups.

As of now you can’t buy an iPhone 14 from Apple’s website you have to buy it from eBay or your Cellular Provider. I will probably keep this phone for 1 more year unless iOS 19 is awesome!
