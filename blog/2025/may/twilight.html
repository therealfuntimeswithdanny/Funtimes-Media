Twilight - A video sharing platfrom built on atproto

Published on May 1st, 2025 | #Social Media #Twilight

Twilight is here it’s a video sharing platform built on the atproto (@protocol) is one of the hundred Tik Tok replacements.
Since it’s built on the atproto you can set up the app in two simple steps!

1️, Download the app from the App Store here
Google Play Store is coming soon!

2, Sign in with your Bluesky account
I was having issues signing in with 2FA turned on (I did have to turn it off to test the app)

You can do all the things you can on Bluesky in the Twilight App! Like, Comment, Share, Follow, Upload and edit your profile all in one app!
Its clean user interface makes it easy to navigate! One of the things I'm surprised at is the fact you can view notifications in the app.
The app also adapts to your network/internet speeds. As shown in the screenshot below and shown at the top left corner of any video.
