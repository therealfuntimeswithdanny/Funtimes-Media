The downfall of Threads by Meta

Meta's Threads app, launched in July 2023, i had a surge in popularity, quickly gaining over 100 million users. However, it soon faced a significant decline. Here are some key reasons for its downfall:

1. Limited Functionality: Threads lacked many features that made Instagram popular, such as filters, editing tools, and stickers. This limited its appeal compared to its parent app.

2. Lack of Innovation: Despite a few new features, Threads failed to innovate and add exciting capabilities over time. This led to a loss of user interest.

3. User Retention Issues: Threads struggled with retaining users. Daily active users dropped by over 80% within a few months.

4. Competition: Other social media platforms like Snapchat and Twitter continued to dominate, making it difficult for Threads to establish a solid user base.

Despite its initial success, these factors contributed to Threads' inability to sustain its user base and led to its downfall. With Mrwhosetheboss giving Threads a 8/10 fail
