Skylight Social is here a new way to watch short videos!

Published on April 20, 2025 | #SocialMedia #SkylightSocial #Bluesky

Skylight Social is here a tik tok replacement developed on the AT Protocol, all you have to do is download the app and sign in with Bluesky! All your followers and videos are there.

I have never been into tik tok or youtube shorts, but now I do watch them. I just don't post. But with Skylight that changed their easy-to-use app and built in video editor makes it easy to edit, publish and share your videos!

SInce Skylight is powered by the AT Protocol all your videos you upload are on your Bluesky Profile same with like count and comments. Now as of writing there is no web app sadly. Tori (buildwithtori.com) and her team are working hard on Skylight so drop them a follow!
