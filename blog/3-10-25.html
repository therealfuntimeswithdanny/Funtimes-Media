What is Alexa?

Amazon Alexa is a voice-controlled virtual assistant developed by Amazon. It can perform tasks like playing music, setting alarms, controlling smart home devices, and providing weather updates. Users interact with Alexa through Amazon Echo devices and other compatible gadgets. Alexa is designed to learn from users' preferences and become more personalized over time.
Alexa uses language processing to understand and respond to user queries. It can also integrate with various third-party services, expanding its functionality beyond Amazon's ecosystem.
