Funtimes Media announces MBDIO, The easiest way to make your bio page for free without coding!

Published on April 23, 2025 | #SocialMedia #Bluesky

Funtimes Media announces MBDIO, The easiest way to make your bio page for free without coding! Profile Photo, Social Links, Newsletter Sign Up and Bio all on a nice clean design!

The MBDIO is made for anyone to make an about me page similar to LinkTree you can link your social links. As of writing MBDIO supports Instagram, Twitch, Youtube, Start Page, Website, Reddit, Bluesky! According to Funtimes Media They will not support links to LinkTree and Twitter (X).

How to get verified, on MBDIO like Bluesky you can get a Blue check mark Next to your Name, you can get this by following Funtimes Media or Danny Morrisey on Bluesky or help work with Funtimes Media by helping with development and advertising!

You can get started at mbdio.uk or madebydanny.uk/bio !
